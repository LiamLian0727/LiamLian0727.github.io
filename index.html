Lian Shijie (连仕杰)

Google Scholar  /  Github

Shijie Lian studied under Prof. Laurence T. Yang and Associate Prof. Hua Li. He expects to receive his B.S. degree in Software Engineering from Hainan University in 2024 and will be admitted to Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) for PhD study. His research interests are in computer vision and machine learning, especially focusing on underwater environment and image segmentation.
